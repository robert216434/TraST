Introduction

Welcome to the Home page of TraSt site! Here you will learn the traffic rules in consultation with the current legislation of the country whose rules and traffic signs you want to study, you can compare the traffic signs of different countries to see a differences / similarities, the possibility to face tests for obtaining driving licenses in Romania, all this will be able to provide some points for a progressive monitoring of learning work, with a cat learned the more it is more likely to reach the ranking available on the page Ranking where it is in the top 10 they are more diligent users. This page talks about details related to the functionality of the site.

Authentification and Register

To access this site's facilities ,like Profile,Legislation,Tests,Road signs you will have to be logged in. You don't know how to do it?
It's very simple! In the navigation bar of the site you will have to access the "Authentification" button.When you have entered that page ,all you have to do is to write your username and password in those fields.
You don't have an account?
No problem! When you are on the "Authentification" page ,on the bottom of the page you will have the "Sign In" button, access it and you will be directed on a new page where you will have to write some informations about you in some fields(basic information).

Select Country , Legislation and Road signs

The "Select country" button will let you select the country of which's rules you want to study.(Atention! This button doesn't modify the language too! For this facility you will have the language button at your disposal,located on the right side of the navigation bar!)
To access the "Legislation" part you will have to choose firstly the category of the driver license you want to study .
When you access the "Road signs" page, you must select a sign category,then you will have to press on the image of the sign which you are interested in , then you will be redirected on another page where you will have the details about that sign.

Tests

This section is available only for the country of Romania, and in the following versions will be added the possibility to do tests for the country of England. In this section you can take tests to obtain a driver's license for all existing categories in force in Romania: A, B, C, D and E. For category A you must answer 17 questions correctly and for category E you must answer correctly 11 questions, for the other categories is the standard of 21 correct questions for promotion. After passing a test the score will be added to your account, you can see the score in the Profile section or if you are diligent you can see it in the Ranking section where the top 10 most diligent users of the TraST site are displayed.

Profile and Ranking

First of all, in order to see your ranking and profile, it is obvious that you need to log in. Related to your profile, here you will be able to see the details related to your account, such as the score, username, first name, address, city, telephone number, date of birth, etc.
