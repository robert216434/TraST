Introduction

Welcome to the Home page of TraSt site! Here you will learn traffic circulation rules accorded to the current legislation of the country which you will choose to study. On this page you cand see explications about this site's functionality.

Authentification and Register

To access this site's facilities ,like Profile,Legislation,Tests,Road signs you will have to be logged in. You don't know how to do it?
It's very simple! In the navigation bar of the site you will have to access the "Authentification" button.When you have entered that page ,all you have to do is to write your username and password in those fields.
You don't have an account?
No problem! When you are on the "Authentification" page ,on the bottom of the page you will have the "Sign In" button, access it and you will be directed on a new page where you will have to write some informations about you in some fields(basic information).

Select Country , Legislation and Road signs

The "Select country" button will let you select the country of which's rules you want to study.(Atention! This button doesn't modify the language too! For this facility you will have the language button at your disposal,located on the right side of the navigation bar!)
To access the "Legislation" part you will have to choose firstly the category of the driver license you want to study .
When you access the "Road signs" page, you must select a sign category,then you will have to press on the image of the sign which you are interested in , then you will be redirected on another page where you will have the details about that sign.

Profile and Ranking

Firstly, to see the rankings, it's not necessary to be logged in ,but it's quite obivous that to see your profile you'll have to be logged in. About your profile, there you can see details related to your person (user details) and details about how many chapters you have read,or how many tests you passed,etc.
